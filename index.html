About Webster University

Founded in 1915, Webster University is a global institution offering high-quality education with a strong emphasis on experiential learning and community engagement. With its main campus in St. Louis, Missouri, Webster provides students a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs, empowering them to pursue their passions across diverse fields.

Brand Evidence Notes

For the Midterm Exam submission on Canvas, here is a summary of the brand evidence I followed for this midterm:

  • Color names: I used Webster Blue (#024890) as a primary color and Webster Gold (#f5b706) as an accent. I found these on page 9 of the Webster Brand Guidelines.
  • Typography: I used Franklin Gothic Book for body, Franklin Gothic Medium/Demi for headings. I found this font on page 8 of the Webster Brand Guidelines.
  • Logo rule: I used the official Webster logo, maintained proportions, and kept clear space around it. I found this requirement on page 11 of the Webster Brand Guidelines.
  • Image/photography rule: I chose images that were clear, well-lit, and look natural. I made sure they reflect Webster authentically as stated on page 20 of Webster Brand Guidelines.
